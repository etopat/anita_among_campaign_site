Our Vision for 2026

Build a just, inclusive, and economically empowered Uganda where every woman, youth, and rural citizen has a voice and opportunity to thrive.

As your servant leader, I commit to these core priorities:

Economic Empowerment & Jobs

  • Expand commercial farming initiatives (like Bukedea farms)
  • Promote entrepreneurship among women and youth
  • Build partnerships for vocational skills and SME financing

Education & Health

  • Expand access to healthcare through rural health facilities
  • Scale school infrastructure and vocational education
  • Promote youth health including vaccination & HIV awareness

Women Leadership

  • Champion rural women in local governance
  • Ensure party positions and policies reflect women's priorities

Infrastructure & Market Access
